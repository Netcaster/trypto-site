🏛️ ARIA Resort & Casino · Las Vegas, Nevada

Majestra & NCORE Unite 2027

Experiential Design, Hospitality Alignment & Global Engagement

Majestra is proud to formally support and participate in the ongoing development, experiential design, hospitality alignment, and strategic deployment planning for NCORE Unite 2027 — a next-generation global civic engagement, technology, hospitality, and institutional collaboration platform scheduled to convene in Las Vegas, Nevada.

As part of its engagement, Majestra has played a meaningful role in helping shape the elevated hospitality, executive networking, experiential programming, and attendee engagement environments envisioned for the NCORE Unite ecosystem, while also assisting in the identification, evaluation, and procurement coordination associated with the event venue hold at ARIA Resort & Casino.

NCORE Unite 2027 has been intentionally designed to operate far beyond the scope of a traditional convention or symposium. The platform combines "High Touch" civic engagement and human-centered collaboration with "High Tech" AI systems, smart infrastructure, multilingual communication, and globally deployable mobile ecosystems designed to facilitate meaningful dialogue, strategic alignment, and measurable societal impact.

Majestra's Engagement Focuses On:

  • Attendee experience architecture
  • Executive hospitality programming
  • Experiential engagement environments
  • Concierge and luxury lifestyle integrations
  • Sponsor and institutional networking opportunities
  • Immersive destination activations
  • Premium hospitality coordination throughout the broader Las Vegas ecosystem

The NCORE Unite Ecosystem:

  • Civic innovation
  • Institutional collaboration
  • Enterprise engagement
  • Hospitality excellence
  • Entertainment
  • AI infrastructure
  • Global communication

In addition to the primary convention activities hosted at ARIA, ongoing discussions continue surrounding expanded experiential environments, executive symposium programming, curated networking activations, luxury hospitality integrations, and additional off-site engagement opportunities throughout Las Vegas intended to further enhance the attendee and stakeholder experience.

Majestra remains committed to helping identify strategic efficiencies, hospitality enhancements, experiential opportunities, and ecosystem partnerships capable of maximizing value creation while preserving the elevated standards, sophistication, and attendee experience envisioned for NCORE Unite 2027.

"

We sincerely appreciate Majestra's early leadership, creativity, hospitality insight, and strategic support in helping shape the vision and experiential framework surrounding NCORE Unite 2027. Their commitment to elevated attendee experience, venue alignment, and executive hospitality integration has already proven highly valuable as we continue building what we believe will become a globally significant civic, technology, and institutional engagement platform.

— Jeff Williams Chairman, TPG Worldwide  ·  President, NCORE Unite
